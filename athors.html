Benefits of Claiming Your Profile Page

MANAGE YOUR PROFILE Update your profile picture, write your bio, and fix your book listings—by joining the website Author Program you’re able to keep the information about yourself up-to-date. PROMOTE YOUR BOOKS Run a giveaway, connect your blog, advertise your books—the Websites Author Program gives you access to the marketing tools you need to build buzz around your books. INTERACT WITH READERS Take questions from readers using Ask The Author, write reviews, and show off your taste in literature. Readers love to learn what books their favorite authors are reading! CLAIM YOUR PROFILE TODAY To apply for the Author Program, you can follow these steps when visiting the desktop version of Websites: Sign in or create an account, and then search for your most popular book via ISBN, ASIN, or title. On the book, click on your author name. Scroll to the bottom of your author profile page. Click “Is this you? Let us know!” to complete and submit the application. We will send you an email when you’re approved within 2 business days. Your login and password will stay the same. Already a Websites Author? Learn how to use the Author Program effectively on our Authors & Advertisers Blog.
